Anabolic French Toast

Description

A healthier, protein packed version of the sweet brunch favorite many of us love without the excessive amount of calories that would hinder a healthy lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup egg whites
  • 1/2 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 4 slices bread
  • 2 tbsp sugar free syrup
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 scoop whey protein
  • 1/2 cup nonfat greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp sugar free pudding mix
  • peanut butter powder

Steps

  1. Preheat a pan to medium/low heat. Whisk together egg whites, pumpkin, sugar free syrup, cinnamon and a pinch of salt. Add egg mixture to a large dish.
    Add bread and soak in egg mixture for at least 5 minutes. This allows the bread to absorb all the egg whites.
  2. Spray the pan with nonstick spray. Cook french toast for 4-5 minutes per side or until golden brown and fluffy. Cooking low and slow is the best way to
    cook the egg whites all the way through and make the french toast thick and fluffy.
  3. While the toast is cooking, mix together yogurt, protein, and pudding mix. If it is too thick, add a couple tablespoons of water or milk.
  4. Top french toast with the cream, peanut butter powder, sugar free syrup, and any other desired toppings.

Nutrition

  • Calories: 460

  • Fiber: 7g
